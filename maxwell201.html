Match Summary

In one of the greatest performances in cricket history, Glenn Maxwell scored a miraculous 201* to guide Australia to victory against Afghanistan in the 2023 World Cup.

Afghanistan had put up a formidable total of 291, and Australia’s chase began disastrously, collapsing to 91/7 in just 18 overs.

It looked like an inevitable Afghan victory — until Maxwell began one of the most extraordinary counter-attacks ever seen.

He was battling severe cramps and barely able to run, yet he unleashed a flurry of boundaries and sixes to keep Australia in the game.

Cramping to the point where he could hardly stand, Maxwell relied on pure timing and placement to destroy the Afghan bowlers.

He built a 202-run partnership with captain Pat Cummins, who contributed just 12 runs — underlining Maxwell's dominance.

Maxwell’s double century came off just 128 balls and featured 21 fours and 10 massive sixes.

It was the first ever double hundred in an ODI run chase — and only the third overall in World Cup history.
