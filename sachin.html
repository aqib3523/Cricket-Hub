Career Stats

  • Matches (Intl.): 664
  • Runs (Intl.): 34,357
  • Centuries (Intl.): 100
  • Test Runs: 15,921
  • ODI Runs: 18,426
  • Highest Score (ODI): 200*
  • Batting Average (Tests): 53.78
  • Batting Average (ODIs): 44.83
  • Wickets (Intl.): 201
  • Career Span: 1989 - 2013

Sachin Tendulkar is widely regarded as the greatest batsman in the history of cricket.

He made his international debut for India at the age of just 16 against Pakistan in 1989.

Known as the "Little Master" and the "God of Cricket," he earned global respect for his elegance and consistency.

Sachin holds the record for most international runs — over 34,000 across all formats.

He was the first player ever to score a double century in One Day Internationals.

In 2010, he achieved this feat against South Africa, scoring 200*.

He has 100 international centuries – 51 in Tests and 49 in ODIs – a record that still stands unmatched.

He was a central figure in Indian cricket’s rise to global prominence during the 1990s and 2000s.

His performances under pressure, like the 1999 World Cup century after his father’s death, became iconic moments of strength.

In 2011, he finally lifted the World Cup trophy on home soil, realizing a dream he'd pursued for over two decades.

He was often the lone warrior in a struggling Indian lineup in his early career.

Despite being a batsman, Sachin picked over 200 wickets with his deceptive spin bowling.

His farewell speech in 2013 at Wankhede Stadium left the entire cricketing world emotional.

He received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award, in 2014 – the first sportsperson to do so.

He inspired a generation of cricketers, including Virat Kohli, who once said Sachin was the reason he started playing cricket.

Even post-retirement, Sachin continues to contribute through mentorship and philanthropic activities.

His humility, discipline, and devotion to the game are as legendary as his records.

For cricket lovers across the world, Sachin isn't just a player — he is an emotion, a symbol of dedication and excellence.
